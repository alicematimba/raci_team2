# Stage 4 — Content & Resource Development

This stage covers developing training materials with subject matter experts and producing digital content.

---

## Developing training element materials with a subject matter expert (SME)

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **A/R** | Education Developer(s) | Owns and drives the collaboration — shapes what the SME contributes and ensures it serves the learning design. SME relationship management is an educational development function. |
| **C** | Informatics Training Lead (for informatics elements) / Lab Training Lead (for lab elements) | Consulted on domain accuracy — reviews the scientific content, not the design process. |
| **I** | Head of LDD | Informed; brought in only if the SME relationship is senior or politically sensitive. |
| **N/A** | Technical Developers | Not involved in content development with SMEs. |

---

## Producing a video or multimedia learning resource

*(Where a Digital Learning Developer is involved)*

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **A/R** | Education Developer(s) | Owns the content specification — writes the script or storyboard, defines the learning intent and signs off the finished product. |
| **R** | Digital Learning Developer | Responsible for the production: filming, editing, animation, interactive build — the technical craft of making the resource. |
| **C** | Informatics Training Lead (for informatics elements) / Lab Training Lead (for lab elements) | Consulted on scientific accuracy of the content. |
| **I** | Head of LDD | Informed on completion, or if there is a significant resource or scope question. |

---

## Adapting existing materials for a new audience or context

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **A/R** | Education Developer(s) | Owns the adaptation — determines what needs to change for the new context and makes the changes. |
| **C** | Informatics Training Lead (for informatics elements) / Lab Training Lead (for lab elements) | Consulted where the adaptation involves substantive domain content changes. |
| **I** | Technical Developers | Informed if any changes affect the technical environment or platform. |
| **I** | Head of LDD | Informed if the adaptation is significant enough to affect the portfolio plan. |
