# Stage 2 — Stakeholder Engagement & Needs Analysis

This stage covers how the team engages with research groups, analyses skills gaps, and agrees scope with faculty sponsors.

---

## Consulting a research group on their training needs

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **A/R** | Head of LDD | Responsible for engagement at research group level — understands the group's overall training needs, leads the institutional relationship on behalf of the LDD team, and is accountable for commitments made on behalf of the team. Engages with the group as a whole: what do they need, is training the right response, and what can the team commit to? For engagements that carry significant institutional or cross-programme implications, the Head of LDD ensures the Associate Director is informed. |
| **R** | Informatics Training Lead (for computational & data elements) / Lab Training Lead (for lab elements) | Responsible for engagement at function and approach level — works with the specific people within the research group who run particular tools, pipelines, analytical workflows or experimental methods. Goes into the detail of what those individuals need to be able to do and whether the approaches being used are trainable and current. |
| **C** | Education Developer(s) | Consulted to understand learning design implications — may join to inform future curriculum scoping but does not lead the engagement. |
| **N/A** | Technical Developers | Not involved at this stage unless a specific technical need is already identified. |

!!! info "Two levels of engagement"
    The Head of LDD engages at research group level — the portfolio view: what does this group need, and what will the team deliver? The Training Leads engage at function and approach level — the depth view: what does the person running a specific pipeline or technique actually need to learn, and are the approaches current and appropriate? Both levels are needed and neither replaces the other. The research group lead may assign specific people to work with the Training Leads on particular methods; that is normal and expected.

## Conducting a skills gap analysis

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **A** | Informatics Training Lead (for computational & data elements) / Lab Training Lead (for lab elements) | Accountable that the analysis is thorough and domain-accurate — signs off the conclusions. |
| **R** | Education Developer(s) | Responsible for designing the data-gathering approach, running it and synthesising findings. The methodology is an educational design task. |
| **C** | Head of LDD | Consulted if findings suggest significant portfolio change. |
| **I** | Technical Developers | Informed if technical implications emerge. |

## Agreeing scope with a faculty sponsor for a new programme

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **A/R** | Head of LDD | Leads and owns the agreement on behalf of the LDD team — a commitment of team resource and reputation. For agreements with significant institutional or cross-programme scope, the Head of LDD ensures the Associate Director is sighted before committing. |
| **C** | Informatics Training Lead (for computational & data elements) / Lab Training Lead (for lab elements) | Consulted on what is realistically deliverable within the domain. |
| **C** | Education Developer(s) | Consulted on design timeline and resource requirements. |
| **I** | Technical Developers | Informed of what has been agreed. |
