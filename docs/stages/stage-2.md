# Stage 2 — Stakeholder Engagement & Needs Analysis

This stage covers how the team engages with research groups, analyses skills gaps, and agrees scope with faculty sponsors.

---

## Consulting a research group on their training needs

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **A** | Head of LDD | Accountable for the overall engagement — owns the institutional relationship and is responsible for any commitments made. Leads or co-leads the conversation when the research group is senior or the ask has strategic or reputational implications. Even where the Head of LDD is not present, they remain accountable for outcomes. |
| **R** | Informatics Training Lead (for computational & data elements) / Lab Training Lead (for lab elements) | Responsible for the domain-specific conversation — understands and articulates what the research group needs in terms of tools, computational approaches or experimental methods, and whether those needs are trainable. Leads the technical and scientific domain discussion. |
| **C** | Education Developer(s) | Consulted to understand learning design implications — may join the conversation but does not lead it. |
| **N/A** | Technical Developers | Not involved at this stage unless a specific technical need is already identified. |

!!! info "Splitting accountability from domain responsibility"
    The Head of LDD holds A because this is an institutional engagement with reputational and resource implications — someone senior must own what is agreed. The Training Leads hold R because the substantive conversation is about domain needs: what do these researchers actually need to be able to do, and is training the right response? Those are questions only the domain Leads can answer credibly. On routine or operational engagements, the Head of LDD may delegate the lead to the Training Lead, but accountability does not transfer.

## Conducting a skills gap analysis

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **A** | Informatics Training Lead (for computational & data elements) / Lab Training Lead (for lab elements) | Accountable that the analysis is thorough and domain-accurate — signs off the conclusions. |
| **R** | Education Developer(s) | Responsible for designing the data-gathering approach, running it and synthesising findings. The methodology is an educational design task. |
| **C** | Head of LDD | Consulted if findings suggest significant portfolio change. |
| **I** | Technical Developers | Informed if technical implications emerge. |

## Agreeing scope with a faculty sponsor for a new programme

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **A/R** | Head of LDD | Leads and owns the agreement — it's a commitment of team resource and reputation. |
| **C** | Informatics Training Lead (for computational & data elements) / Lab Training Lead (for lab elements) | Consulted on what is realistically deliverable within the domain. |
| **C** | Education Developer(s) | Consulted on design timeline and resource requirements. |
| **I** | Technical Developers | Informed of what has been agreed. |
