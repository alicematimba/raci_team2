# Stage 2 — Stakeholder Engagement & Needs Analysis

This stage covers how the team engages with research groups, analyses skills gaps, and agrees scope with faculty sponsors.

---

## Consulting a research group on their training needs

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **A/R** | Informatics Training Lead (for computational & data elements) / Lab Training Lead (for lab elements) | Owns and leads the domain relationship — this is their strategic stakeholder interface, not an educational design function. |
| **C** | Education Developer(s) | May join to understand learning design implications, but the Lead drives the conversation. |
| **I** | Head of LDD | Informed of outcomes; brought in only if the ask is strategically significant. |
| **N/A** | Technical Developers | Not involved at this stage unless a specific technical need is already identified. |

---

## Conducting a skills gap analysis

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **A** | Informatics Training Lead (for computational & data elements) / Lab Training Lead (for lab elements) | Accountable that the analysis is thorough and domain-accurate — signs off the conclusions. |
| **R** | Education Developer(s) | Responsible for designing the data-gathering approach, running it and synthesising findings. The methodology is an educational design task. |
| **C** | Head of LDD | Consulted if findings suggest significant portfolio change. |
| **I** | Technical Developers | Informed if technical implications emerge. |

---

## Agreeing scope with a faculty sponsor for a new programme

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **A/R** | Head of LDD | Leads and owns the agreement — it's a commitment of team resource and reputation. |
| **C** | Informatics Training Lead (for computational & data elements) / Lab Training Lead (for lab elements) | Consulted on what is realistically deliverable within the domain. |
| **C** | Education Developer(s) | Consulted on design timeline and resource requirements. |
| **I** | Technical Developers | Informed of what has been agreed. |
