# Stage 7 — Delivery

This stage covers running live in-person events, managing online programmes, and handling incidents during delivery.

---

## Running a live in-person training event

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **A** | Informatics Training Lead (for informatics elements) / Lab Training Lead (for lab elements) | Accountable for the learner experience on the day. Both JDs confirm the Lead may contribute to delivery where specialist expertise or QA requires senior input — but A sits here regardless. |
| **R** | Education Developer(s) | Responsible for delivering sessions, facilitating activities and managing the room. |
| **R** | Informatics Technical Developer (for informatics elements) / Lab Technical & Operations Developer (for lab elements) | Responsible for technical support during the session. |
| **I** | Head of LDD | Informed; present only if the course has particular institutional significance. |

---

## Managing a live online training event or asynchronous digital programme

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **A/R** | Digital Learning Developer | Owns the learner experience on the platform — monitors, troubleshoots and responds. |
| **C** | Education Developer(s) | Consulted if learner engagement or learning issues arise that may require design changes. |
| **I** | Informatics Training Lead (for informatics elements) / Lab Training Lead (for lab elements) | Informed of learner progress and any domain-specific issues only. |
| **N/A** | Informatics Training Lead | N/A on online platform management — not in their JD. |
| **N/A** | Lab Training Lead | N/A on online platform management — not in their JD. |

!!! warning "Clarification: Training Leads and online delivery"
    The Training Leads' I here is very limited — they need to know if learners are struggling with the scientific content. Platform operation, learner data and digital delivery are entirely outside both JDs.

---

## Handling an incident during delivery — environment fails or practical goes wrong

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **R** | Informatics Technical Developer (for informatics elements) / Lab Technical & Operations Developer (for lab elements) | Responsible for diagnosing and fixing the technical issue in the moment. |
| **A** | Informatics Training Lead (for informatics elements) / Lab Training Lead (for lab elements) | Accountable for managing the situation with learners and deciding how to proceed. Both JDs confirm the Lead acts as escalation point for incidents. |
| **I** | Education Developer(s) | Informed — may need to adjust remaining session activities. |
| **I** | Head of LDD | Informed after the fact; involved in real time only if the incident is serious. |
