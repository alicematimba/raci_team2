# Stage 8 — Evaluation & Continuous Improvement

This stage covers collecting learner feedback, running annual programme reviews, and updating content following feedback or scientific changes.

---

## Collecting and analysing learner feedback

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **A** | Informatics Training Lead (for informatics elements) / Lab Training Lead (for lab elements) | Accountable that the programme responds to what feedback shows — owns the quality cycle. |
| **R** | Education Developer(s) | Responsible for designing the feedback instruments, collecting data and writing the analysis. Feedback methodology is an educational design function. |
| **C** | Head of LDD | Consulted if findings suggest portfolio-level implications. |
| **I** | Technical Developers | Informed if feedback highlights technical issues needing attention. |

---

## Annual programme review

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **A** | Head of LDD | Accountable for the portfolio's health overall. |
| **R** | Informatics Training Lead (for informatics elements) / Lab Training Lead (for lab elements) | Responsible for reviewing their domain's programmes — uptake, outcomes, domain relevance. This is a portfolio-level strategic function that sits with the Lead. |
| **R** | Education Developer(s) | Responsible for pulling together the design evidence: feedback, completion rates, learning outcome achievement. |
| **I** | Technical Developers | Informed of any decisions that affect infrastructure or platforms. |

---

## Updating training element content following feedback or a change in the science

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **A** | Informatics Training Lead (for informatics elements) / Lab Training Lead (for lab elements) | Accountable that the update is scientifically accurate and the domain standard is maintained — approves the changes. |
| **R** | Education Developer(s) | Responsible for making the changes to materials, structure or activities. |
| **R** | Relevant Technical Dev | Responsible for updating any technical components. |
| **I** | Head of LDD | Informed that the course has been updated and is ready to run again. |
