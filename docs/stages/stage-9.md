# Stage 9 — External Partnerships & Institutional Relationships

This stage covers negotiating institutional agreements, collaboratively developing training with external partners, and representing the team externally.

---

## Negotiating a Memorandum of Agreement with another institution

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **A/R** | Head of LDD | Leads the LDD team's involvement in the agreement — a strategic and legal commitment on behalf of the team. Both JDs confirm the Leads contribute to MoAs but do not own them. For MoAs with significant cross-programme or institutional implications, the Associate Director is involved in or briefed on the final terms before sign-off. |
| **C** | Both Training Leads | Consulted on what the partnership would require of their domain. |
| **I** | Education Developers | Informed of what's agreed and what it means for their work. |
| **I** | Technical Developers | Informed of any shared infrastructure or environment implications. |

---

## Collaboratively developing a training event with an external partner institution

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **A** | Head of LDD | Accountable for the quality of Sanger's contribution and the overall partnership. |
| **R** | Education Developer(s) | Responsible for the joint design work — the day-to-day collaboration. |
| **C** | Informatics Training Lead (for computational & data elements) / Lab Training Lead (for lab elements) | Consulted on domain content and accuracy — the C here reflects their expert review function, not design ownership. |
| **I** | Technical Developers | Informed; brought in as R only if joint technical infrastructure is required. |

---

!!! note "External representation"
    When team members represent the team at conferences, events or external forums, the Head of LDD is accountable for what is said on behalf of the LDD team. Whoever attends is responsible for preparing and delivering the contribution, consulting the relevant Training Lead or Education Developer on domain or design content where relevant. The rest of the team is informed of outcomes and any connections made.
