# Scenario 2 — Producing a New FutureLearn MOOC on Infectious Disease Genomics

*Digital learning production — from brief to live course*

---

## Context

> The Head of LDD has agreed with FutureLearn to produce a new six-week online course on infectious disease genomics. The course needs to be accessible to an international audience with varying technical backgrounds. It will include video lectures, interactive exercises, and a discussion forum.

---

## Learning design

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **A** | Head of LDD | Accountable for the course meeting Sanger's quality standards and fulfilling the FutureLearn agreement. |
| **R** | Education Developer(s) | Designs the course structure, writes learning outcomes, commissions and scripts video content, designs all interactive exercises, and manages the SME relationship. |
| **C** | Informatics Training Lead | Consulted on approach validity and content accuracy — reviews scripts and exercises to confirm that what is being taught reflects current, meaningful practice in the field and is appropriate for the audience. This is C, not R: the Education Developer authors; the Lead quality-checks. |
| **N/A** | Lab Training Lead | No involvement — this is an informatics and digital learning course. |
| **N/A** | Lab Technical & Operations Developer | No involvement — no lab or physical infrastructure required. |

---

## Digital content production

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **A/R** | Digital Learning Developer | Owns and executes all technical production — records and edits video lectures, builds interactive content on FutureLearn, manages the course on the platform, and handles all LMS configuration and publishing. |
| **R** | Education Developer(s) | Provides all content (scripts, slides, exercise files) to the Digital Learning Developer and reviews produced materials before publication. |
| **I** | Informatics Technical Developer | Informed only — no technical infrastructure role in a FutureLearn course. |

---

## Launch and live delivery

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **A** | Head of LDD | Accountable for the course going live and Sanger's commitment to FutureLearn being met. |
| **A/R** | Digital Learning Developer | Manages the live course on the platform — monitors enrolments, responds to technical issues, and manages the FutureLearn relationship during the run. |
| **R** | Education Developer(s) | Monitors discussion forums, responds to learner questions on content, and escalates any domain or approach issues to the Informatics Training Lead. |
| **I** | Informatics Training Lead | Informed of course performance and learner feedback. |

---

## What this scenario illustrates

!!! success "Key insight"
    The Digital Learning Developer is the central technical owner for online courses — not the Informatics Technical Developer. The Informatics Training Lead's role is limited to content review (C), not design or production. The Lab Training Lead and Lab Technical & Operations Developer are entirely out of scope.

    This scenario also shows how a course can have N/A entries — the RACI actively confirms who is not involved, which is just as useful as knowing who is.
