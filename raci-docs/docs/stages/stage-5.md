# Stage 5 — Technical Build & Infrastructure

This stage covers building computational environments, developing lab protocols, and setting up training on learning management systems.

---

## Building a computational training environment (e.g. JupyterHub, Galaxy)

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **A** | Informatics Training Lead | Accountable for environment readiness — signs off that it is fit for learners. Per the JD: provides 'assurance of environment readiness prior to course delivery.' |
| **A/R** | Informatics Technical Developer | Accountable and responsible for the build, configuration and technical performance. The JD is explicit: the Informatics Lead is 'not responsible for day-to-day technical implementation.' |
| **C** | Education Developer(s) | Consulted — confirms the environment supports the planned exercises. |
| **N/A** | Lab Training Lead | Not involved — different domain. |
| **N/A** | Lab Technical Developer | Not involved — different domain. |
| **N/A** | Digital Learning Developer | Not involved in computational infrastructure. |

!!! info "Two people hold A — for different things"
    Two people hold A here for different things, which is valid. The Informatics Lead is A on **learner readiness** (the educational question). The Informatics Technical Developer is A/R on the **technical build** (the infrastructure question). These are distinct and do not conflict.

---

## Developing and validating a lab protocol for a new practical

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **A** | Lab Training Lead | Accountable for scientific sign-off and safety — the quality gate. Per the JD: 'own and formally sign off all laboratory risk assessments.' |
| **A/R** | Lab Technical & Operations Dev | Accountable and responsible for the build and technical integrity of the protocol. The Lab Lead is not responsible for day-to-day lab operations. |
| **C** | Education Developer(s) | Consulted on whether the protocol can be delivered within session time and learner level. |
| **N/A** | Informatics Training Lead | Not involved — different domain. |
| **N/A** | Informatics Technical Developer | Not involved — different domain. |

---

## Setting up a training event on the LMS (FutureLearn or internal platform)

| RACI | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
| **A/R** | Digital Learning Developer | Owns and configures the course on the platform — this is their core domain. |
| **C** | Education Developer(s) | Consulted to ensure the platform structure reflects the intended course flow. |
| **I** | Informatics Training Lead (for informatics elements) / Lab Training Lead (for lab elements) | Informed when the course shell is ready for content — their involvement is domain content review, not platform management. |
| **N/A** | Informatics Training Lead | N/A on platform setup unless informatics content is being loaded — informed at most. |
| **N/A** | Lab Training Lead | N/A on platform setup unless lab content is being loaded — informed at most. |

!!! warning "Clarification: Training Leads and LMS"
    Both Training Lead JDs make no mention of LMS, FutureLearn or online platforms. Their I here is limited to knowing their course content is going online. Platform setup, configuration and management are entirely outside their remit.
